NIGHT CITY

Night City is a sprawling, neon-lit metropolis at the heart of Cyberpunk 2077. Known for its towering skyscrapers, bustling streets, and vibrant nightlife, the city is a hub of technology, opportunity, and danger. In this world, mega-corporations hold immense power, and every corner hides a story of ambition, betrayal, or survival. As V, players navigate the city's diverse districts, interact with unforgettable characters, and shape their own destiny in a society where cybernetic enhancements and high-tech weaponry are the norm. Night City never sleeps, offering endless possibilities and challenges for those daring enough to chase their dreams.
