Weapons in Cyberpunk 2077

In Cyberpunk 2077, weapons play a central role in combat, allowing players to adapt their playstyle to a wide variety of challenges. From smart targeting systems to old-school revolvers, the game offers a vast arsenal, each with its own mechanics, lore, and unique feel.

Weapons are divided into three main categories:

  1. Power Weapons
    These are the most conventional weapons and use traditional ballistic technology.
  2. Tech Weapons
    These weapons use electromagnetic technology to fire projectiles at extreme speeds.
  3. Smart Weapons
    These weapons track enemies and automatically shoot at them.

In addition to these categories, there are Iconic Weapons—rare, named guns with special abilities or designs.

Weapon customization is also a key feature. Players can modify guns with attachments like scopes, silencers, and software upgrades to match their build and mission needs.

Whether you're a stealth hacker or a full-auto street samurai, Cyberpunk 2077 ensures that the right weapon is always within reach—if you can find it.

Weapon Manufacturers

In Night City, several major corporations and underground groups produce the weapons you find on the streets. Each manufacturer has its own style, technology, and reputation.

  • Arasaka – High-tech, reliable, and favored by corpo security.
  • Militech – Military-grade firepower, often used by mercenaries.
  • Kang Tao – Specializes in smart weaponry and advanced targeting systems.
  • Budget Arms – Cheap, mass-produced guns for the masses.
  • Tsunami Defense Systems – Known for powerful sniper rifles and shotguns.

A Known Gun: SKIPPY

Skippy is a unique smart pistol in Cyberpunk 2077, known for its talking AI and quirky personality. It automatically targets enemies and offers two firing modes: "Puppy-Loving Pacifist" (aims for legs) and "Stone Cold Killer" (aims for heads). Skippy is both a powerful weapon and a memorable companion during your journey in Night City.
