The Map of Cyberpunk2077

Night City is a futuristic metropolis on California’s coast in Cyberpunk 2077. It draws dreamers, outcasts, and power players alike. Neon-lit skyscrapers rise through the smog, blending technology with human ambition. From the sleek corporate towers of City Center to the gang-run alleys of Watson and wild Badlands, Night City pulses with culture, conflict, and survival. Whether you’re a hacker, mercenary, or corporate player, the city’s dark secrets and dangers challenge everyone. Despite the risks, Night City is a place to reinvent yourself and chase your destiny — a city of neon dreams and harsh realities.
